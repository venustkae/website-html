Habits

  1. Playing Mobile Legends

    2. Playing Mobile Legends is a great way to relieve stress and have fun. It also helps improve strategic thinking, teamwork, and decision-making skills. Plus, it's a good way to bond with friends online.

  2. Waking Up Early

    3. Starting the day early gives more time to accomplish tasks without feeling rushed. It also helps establish a productive routine and improves overall well-being.

  3. Reading Books or Articles

    4. Reading expands knowledge, improves vocabulary, and enhances critical thinking skills. It also keeps the mind active and engaged in learning new things.

  4. Practicing Cybersecurity Awareness

    5. Being mindful of online security is essential, especially for ICT students. Protecting personal data, avoiding phishing scams, and using strong passwords help prevent cyber threats.

  5. Regular Exercise

    6. Staying active through exercise improves physical health, boosts mood, and increases energy levels. Even simple activities like walking or stretching can make a big difference.

  6. Time Management

    7. Organizing tasks and managing time effectively helps in balancing studies, gaming, and other responsibilities. It reduces stress and ensures productivity.

  7. Socializing and Making Friends

    8. Building connections with others improves social skills and emotional well-being. Having supportive friends also helps in personal growth and motivation.

  8. Continuous Learning

    9. Whether through online courses, tutorials, or self-study, learning new skills keeps the mind sharp. It also opens up more opportunities for personal and career growth.

  9. Saving Money

    10. Developing the habit of saving money teaches financial responsibility. It ensures financial stability and helps in achieving future goals.

  10. Practicing Self-Care

    11. Taking time to relax, meditate, or do activities that bring joy is essential for mental health. Self-care helps in maintaining a positive mindset and reducing stress.
